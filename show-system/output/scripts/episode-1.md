[Show Slide 7: Organization Strategy]

Organization is critical. If someone wants a black and grey realism piece, they shouldn't have to scroll through 50 color traditional tattoos to find examples.

Categories matter:
- Style (realism, traditional, black & grey, etc.)
- Placement (sleeve, back piece, small work)
- Theme (portraits, animals, floral, etc.)

Make it easy for them to see: 'Yes, this artist can definitely do what I want.'

[Show Slide 8: Image Optimization]

Technical but super important: image optimization. If each portfolio photo is 5MB, your page will take 30 seconds to load on mobile. Your client is gone in 5 seconds.

Compress your images. Tools like TinyPNG make this easy. Get each image down to 100-300KB without losing visible quality.
